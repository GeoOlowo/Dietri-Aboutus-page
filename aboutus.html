About Us

Dietri is an App that allows users make healthier food choices by providing a basic food nutritional value guide and suggested meal options. Dietri conveniently solves the problem of thinking of healthy meal options to choose from regardless of age and health status. It has easy to navigate features that helps users keep track of their nutrition intake while working with a user selected price range of weekly expenditure. It is aimed at helping users to stay true and consistent with their healthy eating journey and motivating them. Dietri is aimed at being the leading nutritional guide App across the world.
